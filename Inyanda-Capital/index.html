Investment Ethos

At Inyanda Capital, we see the promise in startups and small businesses and their ability to shape a better world. This is why we invest in shariah compliant startups and small businesses that we believe can be a catalyst for positive change.

The investment ethos of Inyanda Capital is to foster innovation and entrepreneurship, create jobs, and drive economic development. Ultimately, we are motivated by a desire to create a brighter future for all by empowering businesses to achieve their full potential.

VALUES-BASED

Shariah Compliance

We adhere to a strict shariah compliant investment philosophy, avoiding any investments in industries deemed non-compliant. (i.e. gambling, alcohol, tobacco and others). Our commitment to providing shariah-compliant investment options sets us apart from other investment companies, and gives our partners peace of mind knowing that their investments align with their beliefs and values.
