Our Story

Kini Quantity Surveyors is a 100% Black owned and managed quantity surveying company that focuses on infrastructure development. Kini Quantity Surveyors primarily focuses on the infrastructure development by providing quantity surveying, project management and property valuation services.

Kini Quantity Surveyors was established in 2011 as predominantly a quantity surveying practice. Over the years the company has evolved into a dynamic multi-disciplinary entity with two divisions focusing on architectural buildings and mining & engineering sectors. Both divisions provides complete project controls services which includes cost estimating & management, project planning & scheduling and risk management.
