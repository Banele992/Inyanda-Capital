Our Story

Inyanda Capital is a 100% black-owned company. It is an intergenerational endeavor, founded on the business principles of former Nafcoc president Sabelo Macingwane and driven by the next generation of Macingwane professionals. This familial dedication underpins their commitment to economic empowerment and maintaining a legacy of sustainable black business ownership.

Run by the siblings: Chulu, Afika and Mwezi, the company has investments and dealings in various sectors that include property, hospitality, minerals and construction. Inyanda is buttressed by successful investment and development of Ntsimbintle Holdings. Success in the mining sector has allowed the next generation to venture into other sectors.

CLEAR & CONCISE

Our Vision

Our dream is Fueled by the desire to support and develop the economic endeavors of businesses owned by the black South African Youth, as they move from a fleeting idea to sustainable and contributive economic solutions. Inyanda at its core is rooted in developing the stories of the young South African entrepreneur, helping them to answer pertinent questions like "Well we seem to have a lot of good ideas on the continent but lack the resources to fund">

VALUES-BASED

Our Mission

The founders cumulatively possess extensive experience spanning over four decades in diverse sectors, such as the Construction Environment, Re-Insurance, financial instrument structuring, financial advisory, retail banking, and hospitality.

Recognizing a compelling need and opportune moment to propel businesses from primary and secondary stages towards a more sophisticated tertiary and institutional phase, Inyanda has consistently positioned itself as a preeminent and fiscally sound investor and strategic collaborator within the realms of Property, FinTech, and Hospitality in the South African market.

ETHICAL & SUPPORTIVE

Our Values

  • Empowerment: We believe in empowering black South African youth and entrepreneurs by providing them with the resources, support, and opportunities to succeed.

  • Excellence: We strive to be the best in everything we do.
  • Collaboration: We believe in the power of collaboration to achieve greater results.
  • Community: We are committed to investing in and supporting the communities in which we operate.
