MINING

Inyanda Coal Mine

Inyanda Capital's investment in a coal mine, while seemingly at odds with global trends favoring renewable energy, reflects a strategic approach to South Africa's current energy landscape. Recognizing the continued reliance on coal-fired power plants for the nation's electricity generation, Inyanda's investment aims to capitalize on the existing demand while also potentially supporting a just transition by ensuring responsible mining practices and contributing to local economic development in coal-producing regions. This investment could also provide Inyanda with valuable experience and resources within the energy sector, potentially positioning them for future investments in cleaner energy technologies as the transition accelerates. However, this decision also carries environmental risks and reputational considerations, requiring Inyanda to demonstrate a strong commitment to sustainability and mitigation efforts.

REAL ESTATE

Peter Vale Shopping Centre

Situated next to the N1 highway and bounded by Rivonia, Bryanston, and Paulshof, Petervale is a bustling hub and situated at the centre of a residential area. The Centre, spanning over 2757m2 (GLA), hosts a blue-chip tenant, Pick n Pay, which has a lease period that expires in 2042. Its strategic location not only draws its attention from the surrounding residential area but also enjoys traffic from nearby schools and taxi rank.

Inyanda Capital's investment in the Peter Vale Shopping Centre is a strategic move that aligns with the company's commitment to creating value through responsible real estate investments. The Centre, located in the heart of Johannesburg's affluent residential areas, offers a unique opportunity to capitalize on the growing demand for retail space in the area.

Situated strategically in the middle of a residential area and not too far from the busy roads like Witkoppen, the center enjoys traffic from the surrounding residents as well as those living upstairs in the center. Foot fall is also gained from near by taxi rank and Petervale Public Clinic.
