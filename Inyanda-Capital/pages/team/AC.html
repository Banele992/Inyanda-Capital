Biography

Possessing a remarkable tenure of more than fifteen years within the Insurance industry, Afika Selemani stands as an adept custodian of Corporate Governance and Risk Assessment expertise. Her distinguished portfolio is further enriched by her specialization in the realms of company analytics and financial modeling, showcasing her acute analytical prowess. Beyond her professional achievements, Afika's fervent commitment lies in fostering the growth of microfinance and microinsurance markets, reflective of her active engagement in shaping industry landscapes.

Currently at the forefront of reinsurance consulting and brokerage services, Afika's influence extends across diverse regions including East Africa, Namibia, Botswana, and Mauritius. Her remarkable journey commenced with the attainment of a postgraduate degree in Actuarial Science from the esteemed University of the Witwatersrand, a pivotal accomplishment that underpins her comprehensive expertise.

A passionate advocate for grassroots academic development, Afika has devoted significant time to mentorship endeavors, particularly within the STEM industry, championing the cause of young black individuals across the nation. Stepping into her role within our team, Afika brings to bear her innate skills, coupled with an unwavering commitment to the cultivation of businesses and entrepreneurs. Notably outspoken, she embraces a profound confidence in the emerging generation of South African business leaders, recognizing their transformative potential to instigate change and ignite the flames of job creation.
